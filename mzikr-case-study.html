mZikr – UI/UX Design

mZikr is a thoughtfully designed mobile application that helps users discover and organize Islamic remembrance (zikr) in a simple and efficient way. By combining personalized recommendations with an intuitive interface, the app makes everyday spiritual practice easier and more enjoyable.

Overview

Zikr App is a mobile application designed to help users maintain consistency in daily remembrance (zikr). The app focuses on simplicity, calm visuals, and habit-building features such as guided zikr sessions and progress tracking. The goal was to create a peaceful digital space that supports spiritual routines without distractions.

Problem

Many users struggle to stay consistent with daily zikr due to: Lack of structure and reminders Overly complex or visually noisy religious apps No clear sense of progress or motivation Existing solutions often overwhelm users with too many features, reducing focus and usability.

Goal

Design a mobile app that: Encourages daily zikr habits (morning & evening) Feels calm, minimal, and spiritually respectful Provides gentle motivation through progress and statistics Is easy to use for all age groups

Target Users

Muslims who want to build or maintain a daily zikr routine Users who prefer simple, distraction-free apps Beginners looking for guidance as well as experienced users

UX Process

1. Research & Inspiration

I analyzed existing zikr and religious apps and identified common pain points: Too many screens and features Poor visual hierarchy Lack of emotional calm

2. Information Architecture

The app was structured into clear sections: Zikr (morning & evening) Progress tracking Statistics overview This ensures users can complete their main task within a few taps.

3. User Flow

Open app → Select zikr Read and complete zikr Track completion automatically View progress in statistics The flow was designed to minimize cognitive load.

UI Design

Color Palette: Soft neutrals and warm accent tones to evoke calm and focus Typography: Clean, readable fonts suitable for Arabic and Latin text Layout: Card-based design for clarity and accessibility Visual Style: Minimal, spiritual, and modern The design avoids unnecessary animations and visual noise to maintain a peaceful experience.

Key Features

Guided morning and evening zikr Progress indicators for completed zikr Simple statistics to encourage consistency Intuitive navigation and clean layout

Outcome

The final design delivers: A calm and focused user experience Clear guidance without overwhelming the user A balance between spiritual content and modern UX principles This project demonstrates my ability to design meaningful, user-centered mobile applications with a strong focus on usability, clarity, and emotional experience.
