FitVibe – UI/UX Design

Fitvibe Fitvibe is a fitness mobile application designed to help users stay motivated and consistent with their workout routines

FitVibe — UX/UI Case Study Overview FitVibe is a fitness mobile app focused on personalized workouts, simple onboarding, and daily progress tracking. The app combines training, nutrition, and activity stats into one clean and motivating experience. Problem Many fitness apps feel overwhelming and generic, which causes users to lose motivation quickly and abandon their routines. Goal Design a fitness app that: Personalizes workouts based on user input Keeps onboarding short and intuitive Motivates users through clear progress tracking Feels modern, clean, and easy to use Solution FitVibe uses a guided onboarding flow, fitness-level–based recommendations, and a clear dashboard that shows daily stats and workouts at a glance. Key Features Personalized workout plans Fitness level selection (beginner–advanced) Daily stats and progress tracking Clean, card-based UI Outcome The final design delivers a simple, motivating fitness experience that supports consistency and habit-building while maintaining a modern visual identity.
