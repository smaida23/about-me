Music Flow App – Case Study

This project presents the design of a music streaming mobile application, developed with a focus on fully intuitive user experience, efficient navigation, and clearly structured content.

The visual identity is based on a dark, gradient color palette, with a consistent use of a purple accent color to highlight key interactions and active states. This approach establishes a clear visual hierarchy and a modern, professional interface.

The user flow includes onboarding with personalization, along with carefully designed Home, Search, Albums, and Now Playing screens, enabling fast content discovery and seamless playback control.

The design follows principles of consistency, accessibility, and scalability, with an emphasis on clean typography, balanced spacing, and reusable UI components. The result is a stable and reliable user experience aligned with contemporary digital product standards.

This project demonstrates the ability to deliver professional UI/UX solutions that support business objectives and enhance overall product quality.
