Smart Spend App – UI/UX Design

This project presents a modern and intuitive money management mobile application designed to help users track expenses, manage accounts, and gain clear insights into their financial activity.

The interface is built around a dark, minimalist theme with subtle gradients, creating a clean and professional look while keeping the focus on essential financial information. Blue accent colors are used to highlight primary actions, active states, and key data, ensuring a clear and consistent visual hierarchy throughout the app.

Key features include transaction tracking and management, a clear and streamlined fund transfer flow, a home screen providing an overview of cards and financial activity, and visual reports that enable easy and transparent spending analysis.

The design is carefully crafted with a focus on clarity, intuitiveness, and visual sophistication, supported by consistent typography, precisely defined spacing, and refined UI components. The result is an elegant and reliable financial application interface that meets the high standards of modern digital products and business environments.
