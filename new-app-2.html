Shop Helper

A comprehensive online shopping platform that brings together food delivery, grocery shopping, and pharmacy services in one seamless mobile experience.

Design Process & Solution

Shop Helper was designed to solve the fragmented experience of using multiple apps for different shopping needs. Users were frustrated with switching between food delivery, grocery, and pharmacy apps, each with different interfaces and payment methods. Our goal was to create a unified platform that streamlines all essential shopping services into one intuitive experience.

Research & Insights

User research revealed several critical pain points: app fatigue from managing multiple shopping applications, inconsistent user experiences across platforms, and difficulty tracking orders from different services. Users expressed strong desire for a single, trusted platform that could handle their daily shopping needs with personalized recommendations and reliable delivery.

Design Approach

We adopted a modular design approach that allows users to seamlessly navigate between different shopping categories while maintaining a consistent interface. The design emphasizes speed and convenience, with smart categorization, personalized recommendations, and a unified checkout system. We implemented a bottom navigation pattern for quick access to main services and used card-based layouts for easy browsing.

Key Features

  • Multi-Service Integration: Food delivery, grocery shopping, and pharmacy services in one app
  • Smart Recommendations: AI-powered suggestions based on purchase history and preferences
  • Unified Cart & Checkout: Single checkout process for items from different categories
  • Real-Time Order Tracking: Live updates for delivery status across all services
  • Quick Reorder: One-tap reordering of frequently purchased items
  • Scheduled Deliveries: Advance planning for grocery and pharmacy needs

Visual Design System

The visual identity uses a vibrant yet trustworthy color palette with distinct color coding for each service category (orange for food, green for grocery, blue for pharmacy). We implemented a clean icon system and consistent typography to maintain visual cohesion while allowing users to quickly identify different service sections. The interface balances information density with breathing room to prevent overwhelming users with multiple service options.

Results & Impact

Shop Helper achieved a 68% increase in user retention compared to single-service apps, with users reporting significant time savings and reduced app fatigue. The unified platform saw a 45% increase in cross-category purchases, demonstrating the value of integrated shopping services. User satisfaction scores improved by 52%, particularly praising the convenience of having all essential shopping needs in one application with consistent experience and reliable delivery across all categories.
